DiabetCare: Prevent and Control Diabetes

National UI/UX Design Competition INSYFEST 2024 (Sep - Oct 2024)

My Role

  • UI/UX Designer

Tools

  • Figma

Project Overview

Diabet Care is a blood sugar level measuring and monitoring application that aims to prevent and reduce the number of diabetes sufferers, especially in Indonesia, and to improve users' lifestyles by promoting healthier habits and reducing the risk of diabetes.

Project Background

In 2021, according to data from the International Diabetes Federation (IDF), Indonesia ranked fifth in the world for diabetes cases, with 19.5 million cases. This number is projected to increase to 28.57 million by 2045, a 47% increase compared to the 19.5 million cases in 2021.

Of the various causes of diabetes mellitus, we found that physical inactivity significantly contributes to diabetes management, which can worsen blood sugar levels. According to the 2022 SDI (Sports Development Index), the sports participation rate in Indonesia was 30.93 percent.

Technological advances in healthcare are improving the quality of care and accessibility for patients through digital applications, wearables, and telemedicine, which enable real-time monitoring and remote consultations. This technology facilitates personalized health management but remains a support, not a substitute, for direct interaction with medical professionals.

Solution

To address this challenge, we designed Diabet Care with the following features:

  • Presenting an application that can help people measure and monitor their blood sugar levels.
  • Providing physical activity features to help reduce the risk of diabetes.
  • Providing easy access to online doctor consultations.

User Target

The target users of the “Diabet Care” application are the general public, especially those experiencing health problems such as Diabetes Mellitus or users who want to prevent diabetes and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Objectives

  • Helping people with diabetes measure and monitor their blood sugar levels independently
  • Raising awareness of the importance of physical activity
  • Encouraging healthier lifestyle changes

Benefits

  • Practical and fast blood sugar monitoring
  • Recommendations and education on healthy lifestyles
  • Improving quality of life

Process

The methodology we used in developing this application is design thinking, an approach that focuses on a deep understanding of users and generates innovative solutions through an iterative, user-centered process. The stages of design thinking include:

Empathize

Identified the challenges of diabetes patients, such as difficulty controlling blood sugar, limited monitoring tools, and lack of accessible guidance.

Define

Defined the core problems as limited access to monitoring devices and reluctance to get screenings, leading to the need for a blood sugar monitoring app.

Ideate

Generated solutions such as PPG-based monitoring via smartphones, activity tracking, healthy lifestyle recommendations, and integration with wearable devices.

Prototype

Designed a prototype of Diabet Care with a simple interface and key features like blood sugar monitoring and activity tracking for user testing.

Testing

Tested the prototype with users to evaluate UI/UX, gather feedback, and refine the design for a more intuitive and efficient experience.
