My Role

  • UI Designer
  • Copywriter

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe Illustrator

Project Overview

Trampah is a waste management app designed to reduce waste accumulation in Semarang and to educate the public on the importance of processing, sorting, and reducing waste. It targets the general public in Semarang.

Project Background

  • The increasing volume of waste in Semarang City.
  • Lack of public awareness and education on waste management.
  • Limited waste management technology.

Solution

To address this challenge, we designed Trampah with:

  • Presenting an application that can help the community manage waste.
  • Providing education for the community on reducing and managing waste.
  • Providing training and education on waste management technology.

User Target

The target users of the “Trampah” application are the general public, ranging from individuals, households, communities or environmental groups, local entrepreneurs who produce waste such as food or craft entrepreneurs, and local governments.
